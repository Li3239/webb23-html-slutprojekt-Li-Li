    Loppis

    Loppis is an eco-friendly secondhand trading platform. By using Loppis, seller can publish a product he wants to sell quickly, easily and it is free. Buyer can look for secondhand fashion product, clothes and shoes, books, furniture and electronics etc.

    Using this APP the seller can take some photos of the thing he wants to sell, describe the item, set a price and then press submit button to publish it. Seller can manage their items. It is possible to edit/delete his own items.

    On the bottom of item page, seller and buyer can leave messages to communicate with each other or they can send e-mail to each other.

    It is possible to share item to social media, like Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. Users can search items with keywords or categories.
